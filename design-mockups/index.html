Features · المميزات
01
Public Announcements الإعلانات

No sign-up needed. Anyone can open Bazeed and browse pharmacy-written offers, vaccine drops, and seasonal health tips.

02
Subscription on Request الاشتراك الشهري

Visitors request the monthly service from inside the app. The pharmacist calls back, creates the account, and enters the patient's medications — the user signs in only after that.
