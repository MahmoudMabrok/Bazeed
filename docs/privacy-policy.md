## English

Bazeed Pharmacy ("we", "us", "our") operates the **Bazeed** mobile app (the "App"). This page explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

### 1. Who we are
- Bazeed Pharmacy — 11th Building, end of Al-Daery Street, Noubaria City, Beheira, Egypt.
- Contact: **us.developer.2023@gmail.com** · **+20 10 4476 7648**

### 2. Information the App collects
The customer App is designed to work **without a user account**. You can browse pharmacy information, health announcements, and contact details without signing in or providing personal data.

| Data | When collected | Purpose | Stored where |
|------|----------------|---------|--------------|
| Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token | When you grant the "Post Notifications" permission | Send health alerts and pharmacy announcements | Google Firebase (anonymous device token) |
| Diagnostic / crash data | Automatically by the Android system if a crash occurs | Improve stability | Google Play Console (aggregated) |
| Pharmacy announcement content | Loaded from our Firestore database | Display content in the App | Google Firestore (read-only for customers) |

We **do not** collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifying information through the customer App.

### 3. Admin app (separate)
A separate "Bazeed — Admin" build exists for pharmacy staff. It requires sign-in with an email and password via Firebase Authentication. That data is processed only to authenticate authorised staff and is not shared with third parties beyond Google as our infrastructure provider.

### 4. Permissions used
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** — to deliver pharmacy announcements as push notifications. You may revoke this in your device settings at any time.

### 5. Third-party services
We use Google Firebase (Cloud Messaging, Firestore, Authentication for admin) as our backend. Firebase processes data subject to Google's privacy practices: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### 6. Children's privacy
The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

### 7. Data retention
- FCM tokens are kept while the App is installed and your permission is granted; uninstalling the App invalidates the token.
- Admin authentication records persist until the account is deleted by the pharmacy.

### 8. Your rights
You may request deletion of any data tied to you (admin accounts, FCM tokens) by emailing **us.developer.2023@gmail.com**. We will respond within 30 days.

### 9. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy as the App evolves. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any change. Continued use of the App means you accept the revised policy.

### 10. Contact
Questions or requests: **us.developer.2023@gmail.com**.
